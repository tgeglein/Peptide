Technique & notes

Universal recon: swab stopper, inject water slowly down the glass wall (not onto the powder), swirl gently — never shake — refrigerate once dissolved.
NAD+ first pin: 15u only. Normal flush/warmth fades in a couple minutes. Anything escalating — spreading hives, breathing changes — stop. Small first dose given prior batch-reaction history.
Site rotation: clockwise around the navel for all SubQ. Mon/Wed/Fri mornings are three pins (MOTS-C + Cartalax + NAD+) — spread them out, don't rush the NAD+ push.
Wednesday is the heavy day: AM stack plus Reta + CJC/IPA + GHK in the evening.
GH timing: CJC/IPA works best fasted, ~2hr after eating and 30min before. The fasted window matters more than the clock time.
